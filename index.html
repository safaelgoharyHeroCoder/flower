Local rose

The Egyptian rose isAn ancient local variety of rose known for its varied flower color (especially red and pink) and distinctive fragrant scent. It is called the "Queen of Flowers" due to its beauty and delicacy. It has many uses, including skin care, making jams and perfumes, and decorating gardens. The local rose is considered an ancient Egyptian cultural symbol, as drawings of it have been found on the monuments of the Pharaohs, reflecting the ancient Egyptians' interest in cultivating it.
